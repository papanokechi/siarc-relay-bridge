\noindent
Dear Prof.\ Adamczewski,

\medskip

Thank you, and please convey our thanks to the referee, for the
careful reading of manuscript JTNB-2400, ``A Complete Arithmetic
Stratification of Degree-2 Polynomial Continued Fractions: The
$\mathcal{F}(2,4)$ Base Case.''

The manuscript proves the Completeness Conjecture for the
$\mathcal{F}(2,4)$ family of degree-2 polynomial continued fractions
and identifies the $24$ transcendental-stratum families as
M\"obius transforms of $\pi$ of the form $K = (a + b\pi)/(c + d\pi)$
with $a,b,c,d\in\mathbb{Z}$, all sharing the structural coefficient
ratio $a_{2}/b_{1}^{2} = -2/9$ and exhibiting Ap\'ery-like recurrence
structure.  The classification proceeds by enumerating all $531{,}441$
degree-$2$ families with integer coefficients bounded by $D = 4$ and
applying a three-stage PSLQ-based pipeline at precision
$\mathrm{dps} = 300$--$500$, yielding the decomposition
\(
  \mathrm{Rat}(113{,}270)\sqcup\mathrm{Log}(0)\sqcup\mathrm{Alg}(0)
  \sqcup\mathrm{Trans}(24)\sqcup\mathrm{Des}(400{,}093)
  = 513{,}387
\)
of the convergent subspace.

In the present submission we attach a revised manuscript in which
all referee comments have been addressed, together with a
point-by-point response below and a change-summary table at the end
of this document.  Sections, line numbers, and theorem labels in this
response refer to the revised manuscript.

We hope the revisions are now satisfactory for publication in
\emph{JTNB} and remain at the editor's and the referee's disposal
for any further clarification.
