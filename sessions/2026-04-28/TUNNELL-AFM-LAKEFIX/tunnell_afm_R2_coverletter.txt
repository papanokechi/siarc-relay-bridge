Title: A Layered, Axiom-Isolated Lean 4 Formalization of the
Congruent Number Problem up to Tunnell's Criterion

This note presents a layered, axiom-isolated Lean 4
formalization of the Congruent Number Problem up to
Tunnell's Criterion. The formalization isolates the
single BSD-conditional axiom
(tunnell_conditional_on_BSD) from all constructive
content, making the logical dependency structure
fully auditable.

The Lean source (954 lines, CongruentStubs.lean)
is publicly available at:
https://github.com/papanokechi/tunnell-cnp-lean4
A permanent Zenodo archive is available at:
https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19834824

The formalization targets Lean v4.14.0 and Mathlib
v4.14.0. To verify: clone the repository and run
lake build.

The manuscript has not been submitted elsewhere and
is not under consideration at any other journal.
