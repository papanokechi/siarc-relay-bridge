Title: A j-invariant-correlated cubic finer-split in polynomial continued-fraction asymptotics
Authors: papanokechi
Abstract: For a polynomial continued fraction generated by an integer polynomial b of degree d, the convergent-error sequence has a WKB asymptotic with leading exponent A_PCF(b), whose sharp PCF-2 Conjecture B4 predicts A_PCF = 2d. We report a degree-3 finer split: R1.1 found rho(log(|j(E_b)|+1), A_fit-6) = -0.691, and R1.3 reproduced it at deep precision with rho = -0.5683, Bonferroni p = 5.0e-5, on 50 cubic families. The four equianharmonic CM families in the j=0 cell, with Weierstrass pattern (1,-3,3,*), hit A = 6 to <= 1e-3. The sharp statement does not extend to degree 4: R13-C shows a real shallow-N j=0 shift, but R13-D deep-WKB gives fam32 delta = -4.55e-4 and matched non-j=0 fam01 delta = -4.60e-4, a 0.24 pooled-stderr difference. We therefore frame the phenomenon as cubic-modular, not as a cross-degree j-law; an Eisenstein/CM explanation is deferred to companion theory work.
