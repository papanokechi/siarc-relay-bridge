Dear Editors of Research in Mathematical Sciences,

I submit "A Hypergeometric Classification of Polynomial Continued
Fraction Spectra with Arithmetic Obstruction Criteria" for your
consideration. The paper establishes a spectral stratification of
polynomial continued fraction (PCF) limit types via a
hypergeometric framework, proving arithmetic obstruction criteria
that determine which strata a given integer PCF family can occupy.
This extends the hypergeometric classification framework for
continued fraction limit types of Lorentzen and Waadeland
(Continued Fractions: Convergence Theory, 2nd ed., 2008) to the
polynomial coefficient setting, and aligns with RMS's published
work on computational and structural number theory.

Supporting numerical scripts are archived at
https://github.com/papanokechi/pcf-spectral-classes
The manuscript has not been published elsewhere and is not under
consideration at any other journal.

Copilot; all claims independently verified by the author.
