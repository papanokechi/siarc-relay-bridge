PROVEN (formal level): For the SIARC PCF Wallis recurrence at
polynomial degree d in [2, 8] and any of the three SIARC
conventions (alpha-direction, symmetric, delta-direction), the
naive Wimp-Zeilberger Newton-polygon ansatz yields the formal
exponent A_naive = 2d - deg_a in {d-1, d, d+1}. This is
Theorem 1.1 of the note. The proof is by direct symbolic
balance analysis on the formal series Q_n ~ exp(A_naive * n^{1/2}
+ ...) ansatz, derived in Section 2 and closed by per-degree
case analysis in Section 3 across the three conventions.

VERIFIED (literature-citation): At d = 2 the formal-level
prediction A_naive = 3 (alpha-direction) coincides with the
LOWER branch of PCF-1 v1.3 (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.19937196),
Section 6 Theorem 5, V_quad family QL01-QL26, verified at 80
algebraic digits. The B-T 1933 Sections 7-9 existence and
factorization machinery (Theorem II, Lemma 10, Fundamental

through C.2.4 (non-resonance match, non-degeneracy match, d-range

STRUCTURAL FRAMING (not closure): At d >= 3 the empirical PCF-2
v1.3 (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.19963298) record R1.1 + R1.3 + Q1
yields A_fit ~ 2d (50/50 cubic representatives, 60/60 quartic
representatives) which lies strictly outside the formal baseline
{d-1, d, d+1}. Proposition 1.2 of the note frames this gap with
two non-mutually-exclusive structural mechanisms - (i') a
borderline-locus condition where the SIARC stratum sits on the
deg_a = 2*deg_b boundary of the Wimp-Zeilberger normal case, and
(ii') a definitional translation under which the empirical
fit-protocol of [PCF-2 v1.3] measures a different exponent than
A_naive. Distinguishing (i') from (ii') is the open content of
Section 6 Q1.

CONJECTURED (Conjecture B4): The note states Conjecture B4 of
[PCF-2 v1.3] (A = 2d for representatives in the SIARC stratum at
each degree d >= 2) verbatim. The note does NOT claim to prove,
partially prove, or refute Conjecture B4. The formal-level
baseline is consistent with - but does not establish - A = 2d.
Section 6 Q4 names the sectorial upgrade gap (Wasow Section X.3
Theorem 11.1, Adams 1928, Turrittin 1955, Immink 1984) that
would, jointly with the closure of (i') or (ii'), turn the
formal-level baseline into a proof of A = 2d on the SIARC
stratum.
