## Sectorial-validity / Stokes-data status

§6 supplies the **multiplicative-structure layer** of the sectorial
solution machinery: given the proper-curve / region partition of §5,
Lemma 9 lets one factor the operator L_n into L_{n−Γ} L_Γ at a
"point of division" in the sequence Q_1, ..., Q_n. The determinant
limits (2 b) + (2 c) carry the same exponential dominants Q_1 + ... +
Q_Γ across the full subregion (1) + ... + (m); the factor operator
L_Γ is constructed as r → ∞ limit (4) of the finite-r expression (3).

§6 does NOT explicitly use the words "Stokes data", "multisummability",
or "sectorial sum". The §6 content is a structural-factorisation
result; the analytic content (formal-to-analytic upgrade of the
formal series e^{Q_j(x)} s_j(x) into actual analytic solutions) is
supplied by §5 Theorem I, and the Borel-summability layer is supplied
by §4 Lemma 8 (cf. Phase E.2 SUBJECT TAG row).

The "pass from formal to analytic solutions" is most directly read
off §5 Theorem I (`[CLAIM-B55]` "completely proper" conclusion),
which is the relay prompt clause (c) result. §6 augments this with
the multiplicative-decomposition information.
