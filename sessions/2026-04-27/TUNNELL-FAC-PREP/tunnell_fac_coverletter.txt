Title: A Layered, Axiom-Isolated Lean 4 Formalization of the
       Congruent Number Problem up to Tunnell's Criterion

This note presents a layered, axiom-isolated Lean 4 formalization
of the Congruent Number Problem up to Tunnell's Criterion. The
formalization is structured to achieve clear separation between
computational content, number-theoretic lemmas, and the single
conditional axiom (BSD conjecture dependence), making the proof
structure fully auditable.

The Lean source contains 954 lines (formal/CongruentStubs.lean).
The formalization carries 0 sorry statements beyond the explicitly
named BSD-conditional axiom (tunnell_conditional_on_BSD), which is
isolated and labeled throughout. Two opaque definitions
(AlgebraicRank, AnalyticRank) provide the rank functions required
to state BSD as a non-vacuous proposition; the axiom non-vacuity
property is itself a theorem of the development
(Theorem 6.1, "Axiom non-vacuity").
