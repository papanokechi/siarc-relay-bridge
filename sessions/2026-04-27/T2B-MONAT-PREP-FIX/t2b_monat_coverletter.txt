Title: The Transcendental Ratio Identity: A Conjecture on
Degree-(2,1) Polynomial Continued Fractions

This note presents an empirical conjecture in the theory
of polynomial continued fractions (PCFs): every
Trans-stratum degree-(2,1) integer PCF satisfies
a_2 / b_1^2 = -2/9, characterised by indicial exponents
{1/3, 2/3} at infinity. The conjecture is supported by
exhaustive PSLQ-based numerical evidence across
approximately 585,000 convergent integer families
verified to 150 decimal places, with zero counterexamples.
A logarithmic anomaly at ratio -1/36 is documented and
shown not to violate the conjecture.
