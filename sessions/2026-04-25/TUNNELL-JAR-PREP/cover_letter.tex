\opening{Dear Editors,}

I am writing to submit the manuscript entitled
\emph{``A Layered, Axiom-Isolated Lean~4 Formalization of the
Congruent Number Problem up to Tunnell's Criterion''}
for consideration for publication in the
\emph{Journal of Automated Reasoning}.

The paper presents a 954-line Lean~4 formalization of the
combinatorial backbone of Tunnell's criterion for the congruent
number problem, organized into six layers with a clean axiom
boundary isolating the Birch and Swinnerton-Dyer conjecture.
The formalization contains zero \texttt{sorry} placeholders and
introduces a formal non-vacuity proof for the axiom boundary.
A companion Python pipeline validates the criterion computationally
against OEIS~A003273 for all squarefree~$n \le 500$.

The manuscript is original work, has not been published previously,
and is not under consideration by any other journal.
This research received no external funding.

Code and artifacts are publicly available at
\url{https://github.com/papanokechi/congruent-relay}.

Thank you for considering this submission.

\closing{Sincerely,}
